Reservations

Our phone reservationists are available from 11am-11pm daily. Please kindly contact reservations@elysian.com outside regular call hours, or if the lines are busy. Reservations can be made up to 30 days in advance, and are required for groups of 5 or more. Please provide at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or changes to your reservation to avoid a $50 cancellation fee.

We offer a 15 minute grace period for late arrivals and only seat complete parties. If your party arrives or is incomplete more than 15 minutes past the reservation time, we will try our best to accommodate you but may need to expedite your dining experience or reschedule it in order to honor the next reservation on the table.


Dress Code

Gentlemen: Please adorn yourselves in tailored suits or elegant evening attire. Dress shoes are required.

Ladies: Please adorn yourselves in elegant gowns or cocktail dresses (Dresses should be of a modest length; please avoid overly short styles.) Dressy heels are required.


General Guidelines:

No athletic wear, shorts, hats, flats or casual footwear will be permitted. Casual fabrics and designs are not permitted.

Ensure that attire is clean, well-fitted, and free of distress

We appreciate your adherence to our dress code, as it enhances the overall dining experience for all our guests. Thank you.
